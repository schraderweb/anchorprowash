Frankfort culture

Frankfort is a village in Benzie County in the U.S. state of Michigan. The population was 551 at the 2020 census, up from 497 at the 2010 census. The village is located within Frankfort Township at the southeast end of Crystal Lake on U.S. Highway 31 at the junction with M-115 west.

Frankfort, Michigan - Photo courtesy of Wiki



In addition to its natural beauty, Frankfort boasts a quaint downtown area with boutique shops, cozy cafes, and delicious restaurants. Visitors can explore the town's historic architecture, including the iconic Point Betsie Lighthouse nearby, which has stood as a symbol of the town's maritime heritage for over 150 years. Whether you're looking for outdoor adventure, a relaxing day by the lake, or a taste of small-town charm, Frankfort, Michigan, offers a delightful escape in a serene lakeside setting.
